What is Aragon (ANT)?

Aragon enables you to manage entire organizations using a blockchain based platform. This makes Aragon organizations more efficient and transparent compared to their traditional counterparties. By making it possible for everyone in the world to govern organisations together, Aragon is enabling the borderless, permissionless creation of value.

Aragon organizations are powered by Ethereum, a blockchain based virtual computing platform. This ensures that your organization will always work, even if a government or malicious third party tries to tamper with it.

The Aragon Network will be the first DAO whose goal is to act as a digital jurisdiction that makes it extremely easy and friendly for organizations, entrepreneurs and investors to operate.

Aragon organizations will be able to be seamlessly upgraded and solve human disputes using a decentralized court system.

The Aragon Network has its own token, ANT, which will be used to govern every single aspect of its functioning.

Purpose of the token
The Aragon Network Token is used to govern all the functions of the Aragon Network. This means that users can vote, using their Aragon tokens, to influence decisions on how the Aragon network shall function. This process towards community governance will gradually happen as the Aragon project matures.
